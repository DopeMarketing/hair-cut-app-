# hair cut app 

## Overview
A mobile app that analyzes user-uploaded photos to determine optimal hair cut timing based on hair growth patterns and desired style maintenance. The app provides personalized recommendations for when to schedule the next haircut.

## Problem
People struggle to know when they need a haircut, often waiting too long or cutting too frequently, leading to inconsistent appearance and wasted money

## Target User
Individuals who want to maintain a consistent hairstyle and optimize their haircut scheduling

## Features
- Photo upload and analysis
- Hair growth tracking over time
- Personalized cut frequency recommendations
- Reminder notifications
- Style preference settings
